Matrices

Array of numbers (m × n = m rows, n columns)

Transpose of a Matrix

Switch rows and columns

\[\begin{matrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \\ 5 & 6 \end{matrix} ^ \intercal = \begin{matrix} 1 & 3 & 5 \\ 2 & 4 & 6 \end{matrix}\]

Property

\[(AB)^\intercal = B^\intercal A^\intercal\]
